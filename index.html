How to Play Simon Says (Online Button Version)
  • Start the Game by pressing any keyboard key or press the Start button.
  • Watch the button sequence and remember the colors.
  • Click the buttons in the same order.
  • Each correct sequence increases your level.
  • If you click a wrong button, the game ends.
  • Your final score is the level you reached.
